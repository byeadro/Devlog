# Devlog — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

## What data we collect

Devlog stores the following data in your personal Firebase account:
- Journal entries you write (content, tags, timestamps)
- Your email address and password for authentication
- Notebooks you create

Devlog stores the following data locally in your browser (Chrome storage):
- Display name
- Draft entries
- Theme and font preferences
- Ambient sound preferences

## What we do NOT do

- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party
- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use cookies or tracking of any kind
- We do not serve ads
- We do not access your browsing history

## Data storage

All journal data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore under your authenticated user account. Each user's data is isolated — no one else can read or write your entries. Your data is yours.

## Data deletion

You can delete individual entries at any time within the extension. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact adrianbond@myembra.com.
